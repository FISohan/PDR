AeroWalk Data Collector

This utility records Pedestrian Dead Reckoning (PDR) trajectories. To plot paths, we require access to the phone's physical motion sensors.

  • Secure Context: Permission will be requested for your Accelerometer, Gyroscope, and Orientation Sensors.
  • Recording: Tap "Start Recording", walk holding the phone flat in your direction of travel, then tap "Stop & Save" to automatically download the logged PDR trajectory path.
