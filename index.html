AeroWalk PDR

Welcome to AeroWalk PDR, a high-fidelity Pedestrian Dead Reckoning app. This application tracks your movement paths in real-time using your phone's acceleration, gyroscope, and magnetometer sensors.

How to use:

  • Sensor Access: Tap the button below to grant permission for motion and orientation sensors.
  • Holding position: Keep your phone flat in your hand pointing in your walking direction for best results.
  • Desktop Simulation: If testing on a computer, use the "Workspace Test Simulator" on the left panel to test walking, turning, and automatic trajectories!
